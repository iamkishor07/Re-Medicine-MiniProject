About

  • Every household has a box of general medicines/ specific medicines that are present for our consumption .
  • While the medicine is purchased, we make it a point to check the date of expiry, however over a period of time we ignore to validate if the medicines available in our house are still under the period of expiry

Solution

  1. We can solve above problem easily by creating an application
  2. By using MINI QR as a tag to the tablet strip
  3. We need to paste MINI QR on tablet strip .
  4. Firstly user need to login to App.
  5. Then need to scan the QR which is previously pasted on strip by the APP. and enter details asked.
  6. User can customize the reminder ,how many times and on what date before the expiry they need to be notified.
  7. As we use MICRO QR it can be easily pasted on tablet strip
  8. While a conventional QR Code requires four or more modules of empty space, a Micro QR Code only needs a two module-wide space.
  9. QR stickers can be reused by pasting them on another strip of tablets and editing the data which is corresponding to the QR stored in database,by rescanning it
